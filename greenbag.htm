ABOUT

Go Green produces India’s first 100% biodegradable substitute to plastics. The idea of creating these products took shape in 2012 with the support of scientists and environmentalists across the world. Inspired by ‘Swach Bharat’, an initiative taken by the Honourable Prime Minister of India Narendra Modi, Go Green is going to hit the Indian market this coming year.
Go Green is not just a brand, it is a Green Technology. Go Green’s products are made from natural starch, vegetable oil derivatives and vegetable waste. These products are non-toxic to the environment, animals and plants. Go Green contains no conventional plastics at all.
Go Green is on a mission to make the world plastic free .Let's take a step ahead to Save our motherland.
